Impact of Terrorism on World Development

What is this app about?

Definition of terrorism
"The threatened or actual use of illegal force and violence by a non-state actor to attain a political, economic, religious, or social goal through fear, coercion, or intimidation."

The idea of the project is to give insightful information about the potential correlation that exists between the development of a country and terrorist attacks occuring in that particular country. To do so, we use the dataset provided by the World Bank along with an open-source global terrorism database.

The former provide information about world development indicators such as CO2 emissions, birth death crude, number of hospital beds, all of this for more than 180 countries from 1960 to 2015. The latter dataset provide information on location, tactics, perpetrators, targets, and outcomes for more than 170'000 terrorist attacks worldwide, from 1970 to 2016.

How to use it?

  1. Close this menu.
  2. Click on the country you are interested in.
  3. The points represent terrorist attacks that happend between 1970 and 2016.
  4. Click on the bottom right button to see details and explore correlations with social and economic indicators.
